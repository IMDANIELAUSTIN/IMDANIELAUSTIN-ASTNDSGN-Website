PBS x ARTBOUND

ROLE: GRAPHIC DESIGNER | 2020
DURATION: APPROX. 14 WEEKS

While studying under Christian Perez-Morin at ArtCenter College of Design our course tasked us at excuting a strong understanding of typgraphic style and fundamentals, the exercising of conceptual thinking and also resolving traditional design problems using critical research. By the end, I was able to present a bespoke logomark and visual identity showcasing my creative and conceptual skill while also showcasing my perspective of strong, legible typographic design. 

Project: PBS Artbound Visual Identity Redesign

Responsibility(s): Creative Direction, Visual Identity Design, Logo Design, Motion Design, Print Design

Objective: Create a visual identity/logo that identifies with Artbound's visual identity while complimenting the design language and branding of PBS.
