ARTCENTER COLLEGE OF DESIGN x SOLE FOLKS

Project: Transdiscinplinary Partnership with Sole Folks | Leimert Park
ROLE: GRAPHIC DESIGNER
DURATION: APPROX. 14 WEEKS
My Deliverables: Brand Guidelines (Print + Digital), Branded Stationary, Visual Identity Design Refinement, Brand Elements (Pattern / Monogram), Retail Signage, and White-label Product Design

While attaining my Bachelor's at ArtCenter College of Design, I had the immense oppurtunity to participate within a Transdiscinplinary course. While America transitioned through the pandemic and political tensions were high the country had to endure it's problematic past and future of racial injustices. The concurrent events led ArtCenter to provide a class that not only served it's students but the surrounding Southern California communities by providing proffesional level design services to help propel local Black and Brown businesses.
As the first inaugural course was hosted, we partnered with Sole Folks, a Los Angeles retail shop and education platform located within the heart Leimert Park — a historical site for Los Angeles' black community. Our goals included providing a commercial-level retail redesign, brand and visual identity refinements, as well as marketing and press materials that would be used for funding promotion and marketing the business after the course is completed. I gained an immense background knowledge of how the operating systems within retail businesses — I even had the honor of my work receiving utilization and syndication via media outlets like CBS News Los Angeles | KCAL.
